AI & automation

Where I use AI on purpose.

Not AI as a feature — AI as the mechanism that removes a human from a loop that didn't need one. Every use case below started with a real operational cost, not a technology looking for a problem.

NLP · WhatsApp · Email
Proof-of-Delivery via NLP

CX agents were calling and emailing every delivery to collect confirmation. Built intelligent journeys on WhatsApp and email with NLP-based intent parsing — the system reads the reply, classifies the POD status, and closes the loop without any human. Adoption went from 7% to 47% in one quarter.

7→47% adoption in one quarter
260+ CX hours saved per month
OCR · ML · Confidence scoring
Invoice Ingestion & Matching

Finance keyed in 200+ invoices a day. OCR extracts line items; confidence scoring decides when to auto-match and when to flag for review. No human in the loop for clean invoices.

200+ invoices/day 50%+ manual effort ↓
ML · Risk scoring · Onboarding
AI Seller Due Diligence

High-risk sellers were slipping through with no flags. Built a 9-category risk scoring engine that runs at onboarding — systematic compliance from day one, no manual review overhead.

~15% sellers flagged/month 9 risk categories
