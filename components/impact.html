Workflow Automation · B2B

Only 22% of orders moved without human intervention. Ops teams were manually assigning suppliers, pricing, and calculating delivery dates — thousands of times a day.

Order Automation Rule Engine

Built rule engines for supplier selection, pricing logic, and delivery date assignment. Orders now flow from placement to supplier without anyone touching them. 1,600+ hours freed every month.

Read the full story ↓
When I joined Moglix, 78% of orders still required a human to select the supplier, set the price, and calculate the delivery date. At thousands of orders a day, that was the single biggest operational bottleneck in the business. I mapped every decision point in the order flow, codified the logic into rule engines — supplier performance scores, pricing tiers, delivery SLAs — and automated the selection end-to-end. Automation went from 22% to 80%. 1,600+ hours freed every month. The engine has run without issues since launch.

AI Product · NLP

POD collection was 100% manual — CX agents chasing every delivery confirmation over calls and emails.

NLP-based POD Collection System

Intelligent journeys across WhatsApp and email with NLP-based parsing. CX stopped chasing. The system did it.

Read the full story ↓
Before this system, every proof of delivery had to be confirmed manually by a CX agent — a phone call or follow-up email for each of hundreds of deliveries a day. The team was spending 260+ hours a month just chasing confirmations. I mapped the entire POD journey, identified WhatsApp and email as the two channels where suppliers already responded, and designed NLP-based parsing that could read unstructured responses and extract confirmation intent. Adoption went from 7% to 47% within the quarter.
