Order Automation Rule Engine

How it was built ↓

Moglix processed 30k order line items a month — each needing a human to find the right supplier, verify pricing, and release an SPO. I mapped 4 order types and automated in phases. Phase 1 tackled RFQ and PaaS orders: auto supplier fetch, credit terms, inventory allocation, and SPO creation with a review screen. Coverage jumped from 22% to 60%.

Phase 2 extended to ARC orders via a governed mapping module — Customer × Material × Supplier ID combinations, with access controls and fail-safes. Full auto-release in 30 seconds. Coverage hit 80%. Opportunity cost equivalent to 50 FTE.

AI Product · NLP

Read the full story ↓
Before this system, every proof of delivery had to be confirmed manually by a CX agent — a phone call or follow-up email for each of hundreds of deliveries a day. The team was spending 260+ hours a month just chasing confirmations. I mapped the entire POD journey, identified WhatsApp and email as the two channels where suppliers already responded, and designed NLP-based parsing that could read unstructured responses and extract confirmation intent. Adoption went from 7% to 47% within the quarter.
