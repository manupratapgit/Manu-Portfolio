Workflow Automation · B2B · Moglix

Order Automation Rule Engine

Built rule engines across 4 order types in 2 phases — auto supplier selection, pricing, PO terms, and SPO release. Orders now go from placement to supplier in 30 seconds. No one touches them.

Phase 1 · RFQ & PaaS 22→60%
Phase 2 · ARC orders 60→80%
How it was built ↓

Moglix processed 30k order line items a month — each needing a human to find the right supplier, verify pricing, and release an SPO. Phase 1 automated RFQ and PaaS orders: supplier fetch, credit terms, inventory allocation, SPO creation with review screen. Coverage jumped from 22% to 60%.

Phase 2 tackled ARC orders via a governed mapping module — Customer × Material × Supplier ID, with access controls and fail-safes. Full auto-release in 30 seconds. Coverage hit 80%. Opportunity cost equivalent to 50 FTE.
